    Add your name and parent's email for OTP access

    PDF opens on the left, AI features on the right

What is Vyonn?

Vyonn is your personal AI tutor - a friendly, intelligent chatbot that understands context, explains concepts clearly, and can even generate interactive visualizations!

  • Conversational and student-friendly
  • Context-aware (knows what page you're on)
  • Generates 3D models and maps on demand
  • Suggests which AI feature to use
  • Available even before configuring app

How to Use Vyonn

  1. 1. Click the floating purple button (bottom-right corner)
  2. 2. Type your question in natural language
  3. 3. Vyonn responds with explanation + actions
  4. 4. Click suggested action buttons (e.g., "Open Smart Explain")
  5. 5. Your query auto-fills in the feature!
