## ğŸš¨ **If You Don't See Clear Buttons:**

### **Possible Reasons:**

1. **Content Not Generated Yet**
   - Clear buttons only appear after generating content
   - Generate content first, then Clear button appears

2. **Wrong Page**
   - Content is cached per page
   - Navigate to the page where you generated content
   - Or generate new content on current page

3. **Content Cleared on Page Change**
   - By design, content clears when you change pages
   - This prevents showing outdated information
